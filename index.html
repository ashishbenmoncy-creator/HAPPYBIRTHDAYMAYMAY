My dearest May May Jaan,

Even though today is your birthday, I honestly feel like I'm the one who received the gift, because I have you in my life.

Thank you for being such a constant source of encouragement, joy, and strength. Just as you've always been there for me, I pray that I can be the same for you as you begin this new year of your life.

Today, you celebrate 24 wonderful years on this earth. I lived 26 years before God brought our paths together, and I could have never imagined that He was preparing such a beautiful, deep, and Christ-centered friendship for us. Looking back, I can only smile at how faithfully He has written our story.

My prayer for you this year is that you would grow even closer to the Lord and continue walking boldly into the vision He has for your life. May this be one of the most defining years you've ever lived a year filled with fresh vision, new gifts, meaningful lessons, faithful friendships, and opportunities beyond anything you expect.

And selfishly, I have one more prayer.

I pray that we continue growing deeper together, my Jaan not just in our friendship, but even more in our love for Jesus. May He always remain at the center of everything we are and everything we hope to become.

Thank you for blessing my life with your presence, your kindness, your laughter, and your friendship. You have become one of God's greatest gifts to me, and I will never stop thanking Him for bringing you into my life.

Forever your Peanut Butter,
Ashi
